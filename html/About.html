John Doe


John born in England in 1990, John’s interest in photography began at the Condé Nast library in London where he worked on the Cecil Beaton archive for a year before university. After four years BA Honors degree in Photography at School of the Art Institute of Chicago, John was awarded a lot of prizes in his student life, and still award.

Upon graduation in 2012, Walker worked as a freelance photographic assistant in London before moving to New York City as a full time assistant to Richard Avedon. Also, John has traveled to many exotic places of the United States to take a lot of photos. When he returned to England, he initially concentrated on wedding, christenic and generally photos with people.

At the age of 25 he shot his first fashion story for Vogue, and has photographed for the British, Italian,and American editions, he has collaborated with famous models, others photographers and magazines all over the world.

2018 saw the opening of John’s ‘JD Photography’ photographic exhibition at South East, London. The exhibition coincided with the publication of his photographs.

Tim lives in London.
